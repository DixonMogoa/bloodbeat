Donate Blood, Save Lives

Every blood donation can save up to 3 lives. Join our community of heroes who make a difference with just one hour of their time.

Why Donate?

Blood is needed every two seconds. Your donation helps patients undergoing surgery, cancer treatment, trauma care, and more.

Eligibility

Most people can donate if they're in good health, weigh at least 110 lbs, and are at least 17 years old (16 with parental consent).

The Process

The entire process takes about an hour. Actual donation time is only 8-10 minutes. You'll get snacks and drinks afterward!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Most people in good health, weighing at least 110 pounds, and at least 17 years old (16 with parental consent) can donate. There are some medical conditions and medications that may affect eligibility.

How often can I donate?

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). Platelet donors can donate more frequently - up to 24 times per year.

Is it safe to donate blood?

Yes, donating blood is safe. New, sterile equipment is used for each donor, so there's no risk of contracting a bloodborne infection.

How long does it take?

The entire process takes about an hour. The actual donation takes only 8-10 minutes. You'll spend about 10 minutes completing the health history questionnaire and about 15 minutes in the refreshment area after donating.
